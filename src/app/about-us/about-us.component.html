Omnibus is a healthcare study group we started that focuses on spreading healthcare knowledge and promoting health education. The name is Latin for “for all” , which conveys our hope and goal. As high school students, we progress by translating concepts in research papers into everyday language so everyone can understand health issues around them and learn to protect themselves. We all hope, with our effort, to make the world a better place.
